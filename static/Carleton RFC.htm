"Real Food is a concept defined by the national organization Real Food Challenge that food should be community-based, ecologically sound, humanely raised, and/or fairly procured. The Real Food Calculatoris a tool used to determine how much of a dining service provider’s food purchases are considered “real food.” Through analyzing this data, suggestions are made for potential shifts in purchasing towards real food.

This website is intended as a tool for Carleton students to input and analyze the data for the real food calculator. For Carleton’s official historical results, see the official RFC website."
